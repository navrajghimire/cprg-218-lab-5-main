Reasons to Keep Dogs as Pets

There are several compelling reasons why humans should consider keeping dogs as pets:

  • Companionship
  • Exercise and Health
  • Stress Reduction
  • Social Connection
  • Security

Dogs provide not only companionship but also encourage physical activity, reduce stress levels, promote social interactions, and enhance security.
